About

"I don't just ETL data. I whisper to it!"
Every Data Engineer's Secret Thought

Inspired by this quote, I'm excited to tell you a bit about myself — and yes, bring the data on my background and experience 😁

I'm Mohamed Bilel HASNI (just call me Bilel 😊), currently working as a Data Engineer at SFEIR Luxembourg. I hold an Engineering degree in Computer Science from the National Engineering School of Sousse, Tunisia.

I'm passionate about the entire data science ecosystem — from data architecture and engineering to analytics and MLOps. While I enjoy exploring the full spectrum, my core strength lies in Data Engineering. In the data world, I proudly consider myself a T-shaped profile — broad across many areas, but deep in my specialization.

Before kicking off my professional career, I completed several Data Engineering internships across diverse industries, including Fintech, Telecommunications, and Healthcare.

My first full-time role was as a Data Scientist at Putnam PHMR (formerly Creativ-Ceutical), where I spent three enriching years in the healthcare industry. Following that, I joined SFEIR, where I've been thriving as a Data Engineer ever since — and loving every minute of it!

If my profile resonates with what you're looking for, feel free to connect with me on LinkedIn or reach out via email at hasnimedbilel@gmail.com — always happy to talk data! 😁
