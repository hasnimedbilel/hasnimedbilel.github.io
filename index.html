About

"In God we trust, All others must Bring Data!"
W. Edwards Deming

With this quote I'm thrilled to tell you about myself and "bring data" to you on my background & experiences.

I'm Mohamed Bilel Hasni (shortly Bilel 😊), Data Engineer at SFEIR Luxembourg. I've obtained my Engineering degree in Computer Science from the National Engineering School of Sousse, Tunisia

Data Science is a multi-disciplinary field, ranging from data architecture, to data engineering, descriptive analytics, predictive analytics, MLOps ...
So I identify myself as someone with interest in all data science related disciplines, but with deep interest in "Data Engineering".
We usually refer to this as a "T" shaped profile.

I had several Data Engineering internships in different industries: Fintech, Telco, Healthcare ...

My first professional experience was with Creativ-Ceutical as a Data Scientist in the Healthcare Industry (3 years).
Straight after, I've been lucky/blessed enough to become part of the great SFEIR community, as a Data Engineer (ongoing).

So far, this was a brief introduction about me.
If you find my profile interesting or want to further discuss it, please don't hesitate to get in touch with me through Linkedin or @mail: hasnimedbilel@gmail.com 😁
